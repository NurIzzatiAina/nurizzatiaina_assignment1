Step by Step Guide

Here are the essential steps for me to become a successful Creative Technologist:

  1. Obtain a degree in New Media Communication at UniMAP.
  2. Always learn and master web development technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  3. Build a portfolio of personal web projects (like this) to showcase my creativity and technical skills.
  4. Practicing my photography skills to create visually appealing content.
  5. Study animation techniques and software such as Adobe After Effects or Blender.
  6. Complete internships or freelance projects from related company to gain real-world experience.
  7. Try attending workshops, courses, or online tutorials to continuously improve my skills.
  8. Network with professionals in the industry through events and social media.
  9. Applying for jobs or freelance gigs that combine development, photography, and animation.
  10. Keep up with the latest trends and technologies in creative tech fields.
